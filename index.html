About Me

DJ Lil Monica is a dynamic force in Ireland's electronic music scene, based in the picturesque west of the country. Renowned for her infectious beats and boundless energy, she's become a premier house music DJ, captivating audiences with seamless mixes.


With a knack for crafting unforgettable musical journeys, DJ Lil Monica frequents both club nights and festival stages. Her sets blend groovy basslines, soulful melodies, and pulsating rhythms, ensuring an electrifying atmosphere that keeps crowds moving late into the night.


Drawing inspiration from house music's rich heritage and contemporary sounds, DJ Lil Monica infuses each performance with a unique flair.


In the vibrant tapestry of Ireland's nightlife, DJ Lil Monica stands out as a luminary, elevating the dance floor with her infectious spirit and unparalleled talent. With her finger on the pulse of the electronic music scene, she continues to push boundaries and spread joy through her electrifying sets.
